The 12 Chinese Zodiac Animals

Explore the mystical world of the twelve zodiac animals.

Rat - Resourceful, industrious, and strategic, Rats are known for their quick thinking and problem-solving abilities.

Ox - Steady, reliable, and hardworking, the Ox is known for their patience and resilience. They are slow to anger but persistent when they set their minds to something.

Tiger - Independent, confident, and assertive, Tigers are known for their leadership skills and their natural instincts.

Rabbit - Graceful, kind, and sympathetic, Rabbits are known for their empathetic nature and their love for beauty and nature.

Dragon - Brave, confident, and powerful, Dragons are known for their leadership skills and their fearless approach to life.

Snake - Mysterious, wise, and insightful, Snakes are known for their analytical skills and their ability to see things from different perspectives.

Horse - Hardworking, independent, and energetic, Horses are known for their determination and their love of freedom.

Goat - Compassionate, charismatic, and creative, Goats are known for their empathy and their ability to see the silver lining in any situation.

Monkey - Clever, mischievous, and curious, Monkeys are known for their sense of humor and their ability to adapt to any situation.

Rooster - Ambitious, resilient, and determined, Roosters are known for their work ethic and their ability to take on any challenge.

Dog - Loyal, protective, and loving, Dogs are known for their unwavering devotion and their ability to understand and express emotions.

Pig - Gentle, compassionate, and generous, Pigs are known for their kindness and their love for food.
